# Hidden Full Simulation

## Principle

There is only one full world simulation.

Part 1 is a restricted view and restricted interaction layer over that full system.

## Meaning

Conceptually, deeper systems may already exist from the beginning:

- source-layer water control
- thermal routing
- atmospheric coupling
- geothermal infrastructure
- deep transport or mining layers

Part 1 does not need to fully expose them.

## Design Constraint

Part 1 problems must still be locally solvable without requiring the player to understand the hidden deeper system.

That means:

- local adaptation must be real
- resilience must matter
- repairs must have meaningful outcomes
- the hidden layer must deepen understanding rather than nullify prior play

## Player Experience

The player should gradually discover that the world is richer than their current model.

Exploration rewards:

- knowledge
- reinterpretation
- better causal understanding
- stronger mental models

Not just loot, keys, or progression tokens.
