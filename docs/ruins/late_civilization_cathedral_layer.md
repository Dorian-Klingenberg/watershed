# Late Civilization Cathedral Layer

## Purpose

This document describes the visible sacred and symbolic layer that later cultures built around older infrastructure.

## Core Character

Late civilization works are:

- grand
- ornate
- cathedral-like
- symbolic
- reverential

They often wrap, monumentalize, or reinterpret earlier infrastructure rather than replacing it.

## Relationship To Deeper Systems

These structures may:

- expose fragments of real infrastructure
- sanctify misunderstood chambers
- turn access wells into temples
- build liturgy around water timing
- preserve partial observations in art and ritual

But they should not be assumed to reflect full technical understanding.

## Present-Day Consequence

Modern people often trust this layer because it is visible, legible, and culturally inherited.
Yet the true source-layer systems are usually elsewhere, older, and less understood.
