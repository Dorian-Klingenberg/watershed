## Puzzle Matrix Context

The puzzle matrix work should be treated as a scenario-generation and evaluation grammar, not just design notes.

Useful interpretation:

- rows define meaningful player-world interactions
- motifs define reusable failure-mode patterns
- goals define success, safety, quality, and discovery checks
- compositions define test scenarios
- tester roles probe the same system through different strategies
- the evidence board records what happened
- the host/human reviewer judges meaning and points

The Builder, Chaos Tester, and Systems Auditor can be understood as distinct testing strategies:

- Builder: tries to complete the objective practically
- Chaos Tester: stresses assumptions and failure boundaries
- Systems Auditor: diagnoses evidence, hidden dependencies, and reproducibility
