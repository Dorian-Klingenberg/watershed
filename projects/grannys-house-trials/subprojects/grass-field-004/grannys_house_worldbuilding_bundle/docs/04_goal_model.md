# 04 — Goal Model

Goals sit above the interaction matrix. They define why the player is changing the system and how success is judged.

## Goal types

| Goal type | Granny example | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Primary goal | Water the terraced garden | Clear task |
| Safety goal | Do not flood Granny's basement | Constraint and tension |
| Quality goal | Avoid washing soil away | Rewards good engineering |
| Discovery goal | Learn what the old mechanism does | World mystery |
| Optional mastery goal | Save flowers, pumpkins, path, wall | Replay/value and humor |
| Failure/comedy goal | Allow recoverable chaos | Experimentation and fun |

## Example trial goals

### Trial 1: Wake the old canal

- Primary: get water from cistern into sturdy canal
- Safety: do not overtop the first canal section
- Discovery: see that ancient system still works
- Failure: release too much water before clearing downstream route

### Trial 2: Water the old terraced garden

- Primary: hydrate all terraces
- Constraint: do not drown lower beds
- Quality: water beds evenly
- Discovery: overflow lips create sequence
- Failure: remove too much blockage and cause uncontrolled overflow

### Trial 3: Reach the new garden

- Primary: deliver water to lower/new garden
- Safety: keep basement dampness below threshold
- Stability: avoid boulder wall collapse
- Quality: avoid excessive erosion
- Failure: dig obvious direct downhill route and flood house zone

## Goal conflict examples

| Goal A | Conflicts with Goal B |
|---|---|
| Get water quickly to new garden | Avoid erosion |
| Clear all weeds | Preserve bank stability |
| Use shortest downhill path | Avoid house foundation |
| Open cistern fully | Keep flow controllable |
| Remove loose rocks | Maintain boulder wall stability |
| Use hidden drain | Avoid unknown underground effects |

## Suggested prototype meters

| Meter | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Garden Watered | Hydration of target beds |
| Basement Dampness | Seepage/flood risk under house |
| Erosion Damage | Loss of soil/hard dirt channel integrity |
| Wall Stability | Loose boulder wall health |
| Flow Control | Whether water stays on intended route |
| Granny Approval | Soft score combining success and domestic chaos |
