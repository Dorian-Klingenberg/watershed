# 00 — Project Brief

## Working names

- Granny's House Trials
- Granny's Garden
- Ancient Water tutorial area

## High concept

Granny's House Trials is the cozy tutorial/proving-ground area for a systems-first ancient-infrastructure game. On the surface, the player is helping Granny water gardens, clear weeds, repair old channels, and improve a cottage property. Underneath that cozy layer, the player is learning that ancient hydraulic systems are coupled, powerful, partially forgotten, and dangerous when misunderstood.

The player should initially feel like they are doing gentle garden chores. Gradually they realize they are waking up a fragment of an old water-management machine.

## Core design sentence

The player must restore and control old water infrastructure to water Granny's terraced garden and lower new garden without flooding her basement.

## Core gameplay principle

**Water is not a resource you use. Water is a force you release.**

The tutorial should communicate that water has momentum, volume, elevation, accumulation, seepage, erosion, and unintended routes. The player should learn through visible cause and effect, including recoverable disasters.

## Current aesthetic direction

Use only the user's original uploaded references and the pre-Thra generated concepts.

Desired mood:

- overgrown ancient aqueducts and canals
- mossy stone, arches, ruins, garden walls
- cozy old cottage/house
- realistic or semi-realistic greenery
- water channels, cisterns, terraces, pools, reflective surfaces
- forgotten infrastructure reclaimed by nature
- domestic, cozy, practical, old-world, damp, green, and tactile

Explicitly avoid unless re-requested:

- Thra
- Dark Crystal styling
- Ulgra
- fantasy-crystal weirdness
- alien creature emphasis

## Tone

Cozy domestic task layer plus systemic chaos.

The correct emotional arc:

1. "This is pleasant. I am helping Granny."
2. "Oh, this old canal still works. That's satisfying."
3. "Wait, water is going somewhere I did not expect."
4. "The garden is watered, but Granny's basement is in danger."
5. "I need to understand the system, not just complete the chore."

## Required comedy/failure note

It must be possible to flood Granny's basement.

Flooding the basement should not always be an instant hard failure. It can be a recoverable, funny, instructive failure state, especially early in the tutorial.
